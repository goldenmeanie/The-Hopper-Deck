Discounts

The Hopper Deck costs $20. We offer the following discounts:

5+

Anyone who orders 5 or more decks will get a discount of 10%. Just use the coupon code "FIVEPLUS" when checking out.

Affiliates

Affiliates who order 10 or more decks can get wholesale pricing. Drop us a line for details. You can save your clients shipping and make a little money on the deal yourself.

Shipping

We ship anywhere in the world through the U.S. Postal Service. Packages go out first class mail with a delivery confirmation number. You will get an email from us at the email address designated in your PayPal account when we package your order. We ship to the address you have on file with PayPal so please make sure that it is correct before you place your order.

How soon?

Batches of decks go out every other day or so. Occasionally a busy weekend, a Cert, or time with our families will introduce a short delay but we try to be prompt. If you haven't heard from us in three days, please feel free to drop us a line.

Domestic shipping seems to take a few days to a week. International shipping takes longer.

APO/FPO

We can ship to APO/FPO addresses. However our mailing software likes there to be at least a few real words in the address. So if yours is all acronyms, please write out the “street address” part. When picking the country, remember that APO/FPO are considered US addresses.

That said, if we have any questions we’ll email you!
